account Verification

All bitcoin providers must comply to a "Know your customer" policy for payments through iDEAL. New customers, ordering over 50 euros per account (total, per week), need to have their bank account number verified before we can send the bitcoins. This process is only required once and takes just a few minutes. The last step in the verification process consists of making a bank transfer through online banking. We receive this bank transfer instantaneously if you have an ING, ABN, SNS or bunq bank account, or within a few hours if you use another bank. Belgian transfers always take at least a few days.

You can verify your account number beforehand below, but you can also place an order and go through the verification process later. Click here for more information on the verification process.
